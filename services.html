Asked Questions.

What’s required from me to get my Project?

Design Brief is the prerequisite with which any engagement needs to start. Once the design discussion is done, we will move to the next stage of the functionality of design. for Front-End development, Source file from Figma, Sketch and Adobe XD or developer handoff link.

What’s the turnaround time for starting a new project?

  1. We set a start date

  2. We take the project brief

  3. Send you a cost estimate

  4. Once we settle the down payment. From there onwards up to 2 days or weeks so.

How long it will take to get an estimate from you?

Once the brief and design idea has been discussed, please give me 24 working hours to get back to you with a brief estimate.

What is an average project cost?

It’s very dynamic and fixed as per the budget! There are no cost restrictions in new collaborations. New/redesign 1-page project as the minimum. This helps me provide the highest standard of work across services and keep it dynamic.

Can we start the design without User flow and wireframes?

If the project is small then yes and we do not recommend eliminating them. It is always beneficial and cost-effective to have everything tweaked on the wireframing stage as it helps with fewer corrections and revisions.
